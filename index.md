## About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at <a href="https://mipskku.wixsite.com/mipl">Medical Image Processing (MIP)</a> Lab at Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU), advised by Professor <a href="https://hyunjinpark.blogspot.com/2010/02/hyunjin-park.html">Hyunjin Park</a>. I am expected to graduate in February 2027. My research focuses on medical image analysis with machine learning, particularly in medical image translation and segmentation.
